Bringing Awareness to Drowning Prevention

On November 30, 2013, Jake Roarke Morrison passed away in a tragic drowning accident.

The Live Like Jake Foundation was created to raise awareness for drowning prevention and provide swim lesson scholarships to those who cannot afford them as well as provide financial and emotional support to families who have lost a loved one or have a child with critical care needs.

Learn More

We provide swim lessons to children in need.

We assist families that are unable to cover the full cost of survival swim lessons for their children. We have provided over 1,700 scholarships for ISR self-rescue swim lessons in 37 states!
